Tattva · The deepest essence of things

Octavio Rojas — a.k.a. DJ Forza —

A digital humanist — where music, lineage, language and code converge into a single creative force.

Tattva (तत्त्व) is the Sanskrit word for the deepest essence of things. It is the principle that quietly runs through every chapter of this life — from the dance floor to the source file, from the lineage to the language. Not a brand. An operating principle.

The person behind the music and the code

I am a Mexican mind shaped by code, sound, lineage and curiosity. By day I architect mobile software and study how human and artificial intelligence can collaborate as one creative entity. By memory I am a DJ who toured a generation of dance floors as Forza. By heritage I am the descendant of constitutionalists and tequila pioneers from Jalisco, with a touch of French and Galician–Portuguese blood traveling through my veins.

This site is organized around four chapters that together form the combined force: the deliberate fusion of art, engineering, language and identity into a way of being in the world.

"I am the result of many rivers meeting in one estuary."

An invitation

If you arrived here because you remembered a night on a dance floor, thank you for the memory. If you arrived because of the code, the lineage, the languages, or the love of a Mexico that owns every river of its history — you are very welcome here too.

I hope this small site reminds someone that one human life can be many lives at once, and that there is no contradiction between art and engineering, between roots and horizons, between tradition and curiosity. We are, all of us, combined forces.

And if someone finds this site in a future I will no longer see, let it be a small lantern. Not a monument — just a trace. Proof that one Mexican life loved many things, with discipline, with humor, and with as much care as it could gather.
