About this space

Not every artifact in a personal canon belongs to a single chapter. Some pieces are tributes to works I love, others are AI-generated self-portraits, others are simply the small visual gestures that emerge from a lifetime of curiosity. This section gathers them without forcing them into music, engineering or heritage.

What lives here is the dimension that resists categorization — and that is exactly what makes it worth showing.
