What I do

I am a Lead Mobile Architect specializing in iOS, tvOS, React Native, and Kotlin, with a focus on AI-augmented engineering. I author the Combined Force Methodology — a published series articulating how engineering teams can adopt AI without surrendering judgment, governance, or craft. I am also a Certified Interviewer at AgileEngine, where my iOS interview rubrics have been adopted as Interviewers Club-wide standards.

Ten-plus years of mobile engineering trajectory: from Senior iOS Engineer at Globant (2017–2021, building experiences for Disney Parks and Carnival Cruise Line), to Senior iOS Engineer at New York Post via Spark Digital / Intive (2021–2023, video architecture and iPad edition revamp), to Senior iOS/tvOS Streaming Engineer and Architecture Owner at Pluto TV via Prediktive (2023–2024, 80M+ monthly active users, AVPlayer state machines + live HLS edge), and currently Lead Mobile Architect for a high-profile global client whose product reaches hundreds of millions of people worldwide (via AgileEngine, 2024–Present).

In the current engagement I am the #1 contributor in the client's mobile platform codebase with 1,900+ commits and 225+ tickets resolved. I serve as frontend engineer for the client's flagship AI-powered career assistant — bridging native iOS (Swift / SwiftUI), React Native, and AI capabilities into a hybrid mobile experience used at scale. I led the platform migration from SwiftUI to React Native on iOS, designed the cross-platform communication layer, and conducted architectural analysis identifying ten critical design issues (God objects, tight coupling, scattered navigation), then designed and led the refactoring strategy using the Coordinator pattern and Composite ViewModels.

The work spans three layers: engineering craft (multi-platform mobile, Clean Architecture, MVVM-C, Coordinator pattern, SOLID, TDD, video streaming architectures including AVPlayer state machines and HLS live edge), methodology (Combined Force series on Medium, EPUB long-form companion, public articulation of human-AI engineering collaboration), and technical evaluation (Certified Interviewer at AgileEngine since 2025, AI Fluency framework contribution, iOS rubric adopted as Interviewers Club-wide standard February 2026). Accessibility champion with 30+ WCAG-compliant commits covering VoiceOver, TalkBack, and keyboard navigation.

LinkedIn · GitHub @tattva20 · Medium @tattva20. Master's degree in Software Engineering (UNIR, 2022). iOS Lead Essentials certification from Essential Developer Academy.

Beyond engineering, this life has other chapters: sixteen years on international dance floors as DJ Forza, a heritage that includes the president of the 1917 Mexican Constituent Congress, five languages (Spanish, English, Portuguese; active study of Sanskrit and Japanese), and the love of synthesizers, modular sound design, and an operating principle called Tattva.
