What I do

I am a Lead Mobile Architect specializing in iOS, tvOS, React Native, and Kotlin, with a focus on AI-augmented engineering. I author the Combined Force Methodology — a published series articulating how engineering teams can adopt AI without surrendering judgment, governance, or craft. I am also a Certified Interviewer at AgileEngine, where my iOS interview rubrics have been adopted as Interviewers Club-wide standards.

The work spans three layers: engineering craft (multi-platform mobile, Clean Architecture, SOLID, TDD), methodology (Combined Force series on Medium, EPUB long-form companion), and technical evaluation (Certified Interviewer, AI Fluency framework contribution).

Beyond engineering, this life has other chapters: sixteen years on international dance floors as DJ Forza, a heritage that includes the president of the 1917 Mexican Constituent Congress, five languages, and the love of synthesizers, Sanskrit, and an operating principle called Tattva.
